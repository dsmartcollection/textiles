Our Responsibilites.

New Products: Developing any product by following buyers design, requirements and basic guidelines in a short period. Pricing / Evaluation: Pricing of any product with basic details available in a very short time. Sample Development: Making development samples / Proto Samples / Counter samples/ Pre Production samples as per buyers requirement’s in a Very short time. Sourcing of Products: Having service agreements with many well reputed Garment Manufacturers and our own, we are able to make any kind of item with high quality items for a very competitive pricing. We are always committed to our delivery commitments with Buyers. Fabrics/Trims Sourcing: Having contact with many Asian and local fabric mills We could source any kind of fabrics and trim with good quality and very competitively. Social Responsibilities: We believe safe work place gives much confidence to the workers. Human rights, occupational health and safety are our main principal. We respect buyer’s code of conduct and always trying for the continuous improvement of the social standard of the workers. Workers opinion and suggestions are considered through workers welfare committee.
